Bluebikes Data Analysis Report (Q1 2023)

Introduction

This report provides a comprehensive overview of the Bluebikes trip data for the first quarter of 2023. The objective is to derive insights about how people use bicycles in Boston and to understand patterns in the data that might be valuable for the business or users.

Data Overview

The dataset contains details of individual bicycle trips, such as trip duration, start and stop times, start and end station details, bike ID, user type, and postal code.

Key Insights

Most Popular Start and End Stations

The most popular station for both starting and ending trips is MIT at Mass Ave / Amherst St.

Busiest Times of Day/Month

The busiest hour for bicycle trips is 17:00 and the busiest day of the month is 3.
